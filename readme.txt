== Description ==

PressTrends makes it incredibly easy to get real value from your data. By combining your WordPress data with Visitor, Social and SEO data, PressTrends provides rich clarity into how to improve your site and reach your goals.

We take a unique approach to analytics by focusing on goals and bringing all available data sets through those goals to provide focus and clear action items you can take to improve your site. Also, PressTrends is currently the only analytics platform specifically designed for WordPress.

We're able to bring data from developers and site owners alike to flush out key insights that influence interactions and growth on the web and we do it all geared around WordPress.

Give PressTrends a try today and let us know your thoughts!

PressTrends requires a PressTrends account. You can create a new account at [PressTrends](http://www.presstrends.io/)

== Installation ==

We've made it incredibly easy to get started. Here is step-by-step guide to getting PressTrends up and running:

1. Upload the PressTrends plugin folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Visit the PressTrends Settings tab to set your site key.
4. That's it! We process report data every 2 hours.
